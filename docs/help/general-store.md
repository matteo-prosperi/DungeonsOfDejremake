# General Store

The General Store is where you turn dungeon loot into gold and gold into gear. It
also identifies unknown items and removes curses.

[Back to Help index](index.md)

## How to play

The store offers four main services:

- **Buy.** Browse weapons, armor, consumables, and utility items. Check an item's
  requirements (stats, guild, alignment) before buying — some gear can only be
  equipped by certain characters.
- **Sell.** Offload loot you don't need. You'll get the store's buy price, which is
  lower than what you'd pay to purchase the same item.
- **Identify.** Items found in the dungeon often show up as unknown. Each paid
  identification improves an item by one step; repeat it until the item is
  completely identified before you can safely equip it.
- **Uncurse / remove curses.** Cursed items can lock themselves to you. The store
  offers a paid uncurse service for equipped cursed items, which moves the item back
  to your inventory.

**Tips:**

- Watch equip requirements. You can buy gear before you qualify, but the Equip
  action is only offered when the character can use it.
- Stock turns over between visits — items come and go as time passes while you
  rest in town, with deeper-floor goods more likely to surface the further you've
  descended. Grab something you want when you see it.

## Differences from the original

- **Spends straight from the bank.** Because your gold is auto-banked in town, the
  store buys and sells directly against your banked balance — no need to withdraw
  cash first.
- **Sold partial-charge items return to stock.** In the original, selling a
  charged item that wasn't full simply removed it — it never came back on the
  shelf. Here it's restocked instead, and (like all shop stock) it reappears
  fully charged, so you can buy it back.
- **A last look at cursed goods.** When a cursed item is sold to the store it stays
  visible for one inspection before it's purged from stock.
