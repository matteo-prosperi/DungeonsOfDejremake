# Seer

The Seer locates monsters and item carriers, and a successful reading can tip
the odds of the dungeon in your favor — for a fee that scales with the target's
depth.

[Back to Help index](index.md)

## How to play

The Seer offers two searches:

- **Locate monsters.** Enter a monster name to ask where that creature may be
  found. A successful search can report an approximate dungeon coordinate,
  rarity in that area, and sometimes how often that creature appears among its
  type.
- **Locate items.** Enter an item name to ask which creatures may carry it.

A successful locate also records a **prediction** for that monster or item. Once
predicted, that target becomes **more likely to appear or drop** on your dungeon
runs until the predicted monster shows up or the predicted item is collected.

The Seer's fee rises steeply with the target monster's level or the item floor,
so searches are a meaningful gold sink — best used when you're hunting a specific
drop or a quest target rather than as a routine purchase.

## Differences from the original

- **Predictions actually change your luck.** In the original, the Seer only gave
  information — it never affected what spawned or dropped. Here, a prediction
  from a successful locate **boosts the spawn chance of that monster or the drop
  chance of that item**, making the Seer a practical tool for targeting specific
  rewards.
- **Reworked fee.** The cost to consult the Seer uses a different formula than the
  original, scaling with the queried monster level or item floor.
- **No "locate other characters."** The original Seer could find other player
  characters in a shared roster. This single-player remake has no such roster, so
  that service is omitted.
