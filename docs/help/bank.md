# Bank

The Bank holds your gold and stored items safely. In this remake gold banking
works quietly in the background.

[Back to Help index](index.md)

## How to play

- Your gold lives in the bank while you're in town, and you can review your balance
  and free item-storage slots here.
- Shops and services spend directly from your banked gold, so there's no routine of
  withdrawing money before you buy something.
- Because town gold stays in the bank, you do not carry banked wealth into the
  dungeon. Gold found during a trip is carried only until you return to town, when
  it is auto-banked.
- The bank screen is also where you deposit inventory items and withdraw stored
  items.

Think of the bank as your running score and your spending account rolled into one:
sell loot, watch the balance climb, and spend it at the
[Guild Hall](guild-hall.md) and [General Store](general-store.md).

## Differences from the original

- **Automatic banking.** The original required you to manually deposit and withdraw
  gold and to physically carry town coin into the dungeon. Here, your gold is
  **auto-banked on entering town** and spent from the bank, so banked gold is not
  carried underground.
- **Item storage remains manual.** The bank still lets you deposit and withdraw
  items; only gold banking is automatic.
