# Library

The Library is your reference shelf: a record of the monsters and items your
saved world has discovered, plus a gallery of the dungeon floors that world has
explored.

[Back to Help index](index.md)

## How to play

The Library has three sections:

- **Monsters.** A bestiary of creatures discovered in this saved world, with the
  details learned about them. Use it to plan for fights you know are coming.
- **Items.** A catalog of items discovered in this saved world, handy for
  comparing gear and remembering what a piece does.
- **Maps.** A read-only view of each dungeon floor explored in this saved world,
  drawn from its map knowledge.

Monster and item entries are listed alphabetically so you can find things
quickly; map entries are listed by level. The Library is a reference only — you
can't take actions from it; it just helps you study what your saved world has
found. Library knowledge and explored maps are shared by all of your characters
in that saved world.

## Differences from the original

- **An explored-maps section.** The original Library covered only monsters and
  items; explored maps could be reviewed from the in-dungeon map. Here, a third
  **Maps** tab lets you look back over any floor explored in this saved world
  from the comfort of town. Only floors with at least one visited tile are listed,
  so the list never spoils how deep the dungeon goes.
