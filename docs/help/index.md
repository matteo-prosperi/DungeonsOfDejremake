# Player Help

Welcome to **Dungeons of Dej'remake** — a from-scratch remake of the 1995
dungeon crawler *Mordor: The Depths of Dejenol*. These pages explain how to play
each screen in the game, and call out where the remake intentionally differs from
the original.

> **New here? Start with the [Quickstart](quickstart.md).**

Every page below is organized the same way:

- **How to play** — practical, spoiler-light guidance for that screen.
- **Differences from the original** — what changed in this remake, so returning
  players know what to expect.

## A note on game data and content

This remake ships **none** of the original game's maps, monsters, items, spells,
art, or audio. You bring your own copy of the original game (or the freely
redistributable shareware), and the data is extracted locally on your device the
first time you play. All artwork, music, and sound effects shipped with the game
are original, AI-generated replacements.

These help pages are written from scratch for this remake. They describe the
remake's behavior in our own words and do **not** reproduce any text from the
original game's manual.
