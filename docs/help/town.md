# Town Hub

The town is your safe base of operations. Between dungeon runs you'll come here to
heal, shop, bank, level up, and stock up before heading back down.

[Back to Help index](index.md)

## How to play

From the town you can reach every service in the game:

- **[Guild Hall](guild-hall.md)** — join guilds, gain levels, take on quests.
- **[General Store](general-store.md)** — buy and sell gear, identify unknown
  items, and remove curses.
- **[Bank](bank.md)** — view your gold and stored items (held safely here).
- **[Tavern](tavern.md)** — recruit AI-controlled allies to fight beside you.
- **[Seer](seer.md)** — locate monsters or items and improve your odds of finding
  what you want in the dungeon.
- **[Library](library.md)** — review the monsters and items you've discovered, and
  look back over the floors you've mapped.
- **[Companion Store](companion-store.md)** — the Confinement, where you buy,
  sell, and manage companion monsters (bind, realign, and identify them).
- **[Hall of Fame](hall-of-fame.md)** — see record-holding feats.

When you're ready to adventure, head to the staircase to enter
[the dungeon](dungeon.md).

**Good habits in town:**

- Sell loot and identify anything unknown before your next dive.
- Top up consumables and round out your loadout.
- Level up at the Guild Hall whenever you've earned enough experience.

## Differences from the original

- **Your gold is auto-banked.** The moment you're in town, carried gold is
  deposited safely. You never carry banked gold into the dungeon, and shops simply
  spend from your banked balance. Gold found during a run is carried until you
  return to town.
- **Death brings you back here.** If you fall in the dungeon you respawn in town —
  there's no body to recover and no morgue visit. (Some penalties still apply; see
  [The Dungeon](dungeon.md).)
- **Drained stats are restored in town.** Stats sapped by monsters are returned
  when you re-enter town, for a price; your guild covers any shortfall.
  Normal death's possible CON loss is handled the same way, so CON is not actually
  lowered.
