# Hall of Fame

The Hall of Fame records a fixed set of standout feats — highest STR, INT,
WIS, CON, CHA, and DEX; deadliest creature defeated; most experience; most
wealth; and mastery of fighting, magic, and thieving — a scoreboard of notable
achievements in your game.

[Back to Help index](index.md)

## How to play

- Visit the Hall of Fame to see the current record holders and their values.
- As you play, your own accomplishments can earn a place on the board when they
  strictly beat the existing record.
- Permanent stat records are checked when those stats change, while experience,
  wealth, and mastery records are checked when you return to town.
- Deadliest Creature is credited only for creatures your character personally
  defeats; companion kills do not claim that record.
- Treat it as a set of long-term goals to chase between dungeon runs.

## Differences from the original

The Hall of Fame works essentially as it did in the original: a **single board
shared by all of your characters**, pre-seeded with the game's original
record-holders, that everyone competes to top. There are no significant gameplay
differences here.
